## Promote to the HSA/FSA audience

100M+ Americans hold an HSA or FSA, and BFCM week is when they're actively hunting for maximum value. Lead your paid creative with the stack; it's an angle no non-eligible competitor can copy.

**Ad 1 — The stack**

- Headline: Stack Your Black Friday Savings
- Primary text: [Insert Your Promo] on [Brand], plus an average of 30%* in pre-tax savings if you're HSA/FSA eligible. Two kinds of savings, one checkout.
- Overlay text: [Promo] + HSA/FSA = your best price of the year
- CTA: Shop Now

**Ad 2 — The reveal**

- Headline: We Accept HSA/FSA
- Primary text: Most people don't know they may be able to use pre-tax HSA/FSA dollars on [Brand]. This Black Friday, qualified customers stack an average of 30%* in savings on top of [Insert Your Promo].
- Overlay text: [Brand] accepts HSA/FSA
- CTA: Learn More

**Ad 3 — The deadline bridge**

- Headline: The Sale Ends. Your Funds Don't Have To
- Primary text: Use your HSA/FSA during [Brand]'s BFCM sale and get more from every pre-tax dollar. FSA funds expire Dec 31, so this is the season to spend them well.
- Overlay text: Sale price + pre-tax dollars, if eligible
- CTA: Shop the Sale
