### **Your Customer Never Received their Letter of Medical Necessity**

If it has been over 48-hours and you have not received a copy of your letter of medical necessity...

**Check Your Spam/Junk**

As a result of email security settings, the initial email could've been flagged as junk/spam. We recommend checking your junk/spam folder and searching for an email from [medical@truemed.com](mailto:medical@truemed.com).

**No Luck?**

If you are unsuccessful in finding the letter in your spam/junk folder, please email [support@truemed.com](mailto:support@truemed.com). Please email us with the same email address you utilized to complete the qualification survey process.

### **Your Customer was not eligible, what now?**

When customers are not eligible (as deemed by an independent provider) they are communicated this via email. In order to receive more in depth guidance about eligibility, please guide these customers to contact [support@truemed.com](mailto:support@truemed.com)

- Backdating: Backdating refers to moving the date on the LMN back in time. The date on the LMN corresponds to the date that the customer completes the clinical intake form (Ie Truemed Survey). The earliest date an LMN can have is the date the intake form was first completed. **Truemed will not backdate letters to dates prior to this time in any circumstance.**

### **Can LMNs be modified after issuance?**

On a case by case basis, Truemed may modify LMNs post issuance. This commonly occurs in response to request by an HSA/FSA administrator to modify the format of the LMN. In all scenarios, these requests should be directed to [support@truemed.com](mailto:support@truemed.com)
