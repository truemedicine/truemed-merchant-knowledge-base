Streamlining access to research-backed health interventions

## The Consumer Reality

More than 9 our of 10 are estimated to be affected by one or more conditions including: overweight, obesity, respiratory conditions, hypertension, chronic joint symptoms, chronic sleep issues, high cholesterol, and heart disease. 

However, in the U.S. less than $0.04 of every $1,00 goes to prevention. The U.S. Healthcare system overwhelmingly prioritizes treatment over prevention. Even preventive care visits often discourage consumers from bringing up concerns at risk of surprise charges being incurred. 

## The Foundation of Truemed

Truemed seeks to make proactive healthcare simple, accessible, and affordable.

From fitness tools to supplements and more, Truemed helps consumers unlock the potential of HSA/FSA funds. 

- **Connects consumers with proven health interventions**.Truemed leverages credible medical research to link health interventions and chronic medical conditions.
- **Streamlines compliance.** Truemed simplifies regulatory hurdles by enabling FSA/HSA reimbursement for nontraditional health expenses—like gym memberships or vitamins—by streamlining the process for qualifying these expenses with a required Letter of Medical Necessity (LMN).

## How Truemed Works

1. **Complete Health Assessment.** Individuals complete a survey regarding medical history. 
2. **Licensed Provider Reviews.** A licensed provider reviews responses to determine eligibility. If eligible, an LMN is provided within 1-2 days. 
3. **Purchase made + use FSA/HSA funds.** Individuals directed to pay with alternative payment and submit to FSA/HSA for reimbursement with provided LMN. Administrators have an option to integrate with Truemed to receive claims data and/or substantiation information to further streamline the process for individuals.
