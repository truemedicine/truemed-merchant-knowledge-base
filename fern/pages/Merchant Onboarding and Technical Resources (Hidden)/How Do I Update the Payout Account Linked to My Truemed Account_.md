***
title: "How Do I Update the Payout Account Linked to My Truemed Account_"---


If you need to change the bank account receiving Truemed payouts, you can update it directly through your **Truemed-linked Stripe account**.

### **Steps to Update Your Bank Account:**

1. Log in to your Truemed Stripe account (the one created during onboarding).

2. Click the **profile icon** in the upper right corner.

3. Select **“Payout Details.”**

4. Click the **edit icon** next to your current bank account information.

5. Enter your new bank account details and save.

> **Note:** Only users with admin access to your Stripe account can update payout information.
