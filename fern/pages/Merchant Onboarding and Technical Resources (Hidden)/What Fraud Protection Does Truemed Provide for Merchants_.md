***
title: "What Fraud Protection Does Truemed Provide for Merchants_"---


Truemed uses Stripe’s industry-standard fraud prevention tools to help protect your store from fraudulent transactions.

Fraud Protection Measures:

- **Stripe Radar:** All transactions go through Stripe Radar, which uses machine learning to flag high-risk activity based on thousands of signals.

- **Truemed Checkout Controls:** Additional verification and logic are built into the Truemed survey and checkout flow, reducing misuse of HSA/FSA cards.

These protections are active by default and do not require configuration on your end.
