---
title: "Will Changing Our Shopify Theme Affect Truemed Payments_"
---

Good news—**changing your Shopify theme will not affect Truemed payments** as long as you're staying on the same Shopify account. The backend payment integration is tied to your store account, not your theme.

### **What Might Be Affected:**

In some cases, the **Truemed eligibility widget** (the callout that appears on product pages) may disappear after a theme update.

### **How to Fix It:**

If the widget no longer appears on your product pages after switching themes, reach out to our team and we’ll help you re-embed it.

Payments, surveys, and order tracking will continue working normally regardless of your theme.

Need help after a theme change? [Contact support](https://support.truemed.com/en/articles/6331777#) and we’ll get you back up and running quickly.
