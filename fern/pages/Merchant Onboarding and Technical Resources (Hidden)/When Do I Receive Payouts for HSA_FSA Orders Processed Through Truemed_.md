---
title: "When Do I Receive Payouts for HSA_FSA Orders Processed Through Truemed_"---


Payouts for Truemed orders are processed via **Stripe** and are deposited into the **bank account** you connected during onboarding.

### **Payout Timing:**

- **Payouts typically settle within 48 hours** of the transaction.

- This follows a **rolling 2-day schedule**, similar to standard Shopify payouts.

You can view your payout history, upcoming transfers, and connected bank account by logging into your [Truemed merchant portal](https://app.truemed.com/).
