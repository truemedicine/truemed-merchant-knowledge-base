---
title: "What Should I Do If I Can’t Find an Order in Shopify_"
---

If you’re having trouble locating a Truemed order in your Shopify dashboard, follow these steps:

### **1. Start With the Shopify Order ID**

Log in to [app.truemed.com](http://app.truemed.com) and download your order CSV. This file includes all Truemed transactions along with their associated **Shopify Order IDs**.

### **2. Search Shopify by Order ID**

Use the **Shopify Order ID** from the CSV to search within your Shopify admin under **Orders**.

> Tip: The Shopify Order ID listed in the CSV may differ slightly in formatting—try searching just the numeric portion if you don’t get a match.

### **3. Still Can’t Find It?**

If you still can’t retrieve the order, contact our support team. We’ll help you track it down and verify the transaction.

Need help pulling your order report? [Reach out to our team](https://support.truemed.com/en/articles/6338625#).
