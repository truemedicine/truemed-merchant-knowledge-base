---
title: "What Should I Do If the Stripe Account Owner Has Changed_"
---

If the original Stripe account owner is no longer with your company, **Truemed cannot update ownership or account permissions on your behalf**. These changes must be handled directly with Stripe.

### **Next Steps:**

The new account owner should contact Stripe support directly to update the account. This process typically involves identity verification and may require business documentation.

You can start the process here:\
[Change the Owner of a Stripe Account – Stripe Support](https://support.stripe.com/questions/change-the-owner-of-a-stripe-account)

Once the Stripe account is updated, payouts and integrations with Truemed will continue without interruption.
