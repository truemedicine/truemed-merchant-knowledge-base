---
title: "What Should I Do If Customers Can’t See the Truemed Payment Option at Checkout_"
---

If your customers report that the **Truemed – Pay with HSA/FSA** option isn’t appearing during checkout, here are two common reasons and how to address them:

### **1. Shop Pay Is Enabled**

Truemed is not currently compatible with **Shop Pay**.\
Ask your customer to:

- Click “Check out as guest” instead of using Shop Pay.

- Proceed through the standard Shopify checkout flow.

### **2. There’s a Subscription in the Cart**

Truemed is only available for **one-time purchases**.\
If a subscription product is in the cart, the Truemed payment option will not be shown.

### **Next Steps:**

- Confirm that the cart contains only one-time purchases.

- Make sure Shop Pay is not selected.

- If the issue persists, [contact our support team](https://support.truemed.com/en/articles/6338753#) and we’ll take a closer look.
