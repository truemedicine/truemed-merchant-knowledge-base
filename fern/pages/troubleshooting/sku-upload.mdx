This guide covers how to upload, manage, and troubleshoot product SKUs across all platforms using Truemed. Keeping your product catalog current ensures customers see accurate HSA/FSA eligibility at checkout or during the reimbursement flow.

---

## Why SKU Upload Matters

Every product you sell must be reviewed and categorized by Truemed's compliance team before it can be marked as eligible for HSA/FSA. Products that haven't been reviewed **default to ineligible**. This is the most common reason customers encounter eligibility issues.

---

## When to Upload SKUs

Upload your full product list:

- **Once per quarter** as a routine update
- **Any time you launch new products** or change your catalog
- **If a customer reports** a product showing as ineligible when it should qualify

---

## Preparing Your Product List

### Shopify Merchants

Export your products directly from Shopify:

1. Go to **Shopify Admin > Products**
2. Click **Export** in the upper right
3. Select **"All Products"** and **"Plain CSV File"**
4. Click **Export Products**

<Warning>
Make sure every product variant has a **variant SKU** assigned before exporting.
</Warning>

#### How to Check for Missing SKUs (Shopify)

1. Go to **Products** in your Shopify Admin
2. Open any product and scroll to the **Variants** section
3. Confirm each variant has a value in the **SKU** field
4. If any are blank, add a SKU and save before exporting

<Note>
**Pre-approved merchants:** Ensure all variants have GTINs (barcodes) assigned. Items without GTINs cannot be ingested by our system and will take 1–2 extra days.
</Note>

### WooCommerce Merchants

Export your products from WooCommerce:

1. Go to **WooCommerce > Products**
2. Use the built-in export tool or a plugin like WP All Export
3. Export as CSV with columns: **Name**, **SKU**, **Description**

### API / Other Platforms

Prepare a CSV with the following columns:

| Column | Required | Description |
|--------|----------|-------------|
| **Name** | Yes | Product name |
| **SKU** | Yes | Unique product identifier |
| **Description** | Yes | Brief product description |

<Note>
If your platform uses a different export format, contact [merchants@truemed.com](mailto:merchants@truemed.com) and we'll work with you on a solution.
</Note>

## How to Upload to Truemed

1. Log in to [app.truemed.com](https://app.truemed.com)
2. Click the **Products** tab in the navigation menu
3. Upload your CSV file, or add SKUs individually
4. Each product appears in your SKU table with a status label

<Note>
Both account **Owners** and **Staff** can upload, view, and download SKU data.
</Note>

---

## Understanding SKU Eligibility Status

After upload, each product receives one of the following statuses:

| Status | Meaning | Customer Impact |
|--------|---------|-----------------|
| **Awaiting Review** | Submitted, pending review by Truemed's compliance team | Product appears as ineligible until reviewed |
| **Approved (requires LMN)** | Fully eligible for HSA/FSA with a Letter of Medical Necessity | Customer can pay with HSA/FSA or get reimbursed |
| **Pre-approved** | Eligible and does not require an LMN | Automatic eligibility, no health survey needed |
| **Partial Eligibility** | Only part of the item qualifies | Details included in the LMN |
| **Ineligible** | Not approved for HSA/FSA spending | Customer cannot use HSA/FSA for this item |

## Common Issues & Solutions

### Product Showing as Ineligible at Checkout

**Cause:** The product hasn't been reviewed yet, or your latest catalog hasn't been uploaded.

**Fix:**
1. Upload your full product list to the Truemed Dashboard
2. Wait 1–2 business days for review
3. Check the Products tab for updated status

### CSV Upload Failing

**Cause:** Format mismatch or missing required fields.

**Fix:**
- Shopify merchants: Use the standard Shopify CSV export, not a custom format
- Other platforms: Ensure your CSV includes Name, SKU, and Description columns
- Verify no SKU fields are blank
- If issues persist, email the CSV + your business name to [merchants@truemed.com](mailto:merchants@truemed.com)

### Products Stuck in "Awaiting Review"

**Cause:** High volume of submissions or missing product details.

**Fix:**
- Allow 1–2 business days for standard processing
- If it's been longer, contact [merchants@truemed.com](mailto:merchants@truemed.com) with your business name

### Pre-Approved Products Not Being Ingested (Shopify)

**Cause:** Pre-approved merchants must have GTINs (barcodes) on all product variants.

**Fix:** Add GTINs to all variants in Shopify before exporting. Allow an additional 1–2 days for processing.

## Best Practices

- **Set a quarterly reminder** to upload your full product catalog
- **Upload before launching new products** so they're categorized by the time customers see them
- **Include everything** in your export: samples, gift cards, bundles, branded merch. Our team needs the full picture to categorize accurately.
- **Don't manually tag** products as HSA/FSA eligible on your platform. Eligibility is determined by Truemed's compliance team.

## FAQs

**Can I upload in a format other than CSV?**
CSV is the standard format. If you can't produce a CSV, contact [merchants@truemed.com](mailto:merchants@truemed.com) with details about your platform and we'll work with you.

**Do I need to filter my product list before uploading?**
No. Upload your complete catalog. Truemed categorizes both eligible and ineligible items. We need to know about everything that could appear on a customer receipt.

**How often should I re-upload?**
At minimum, once per quarter. Also re-upload whenever you add new products to your store.

**Can staff members upload SKUs, or only the account owner?**
Both Owners and Staff roles in the Truemed Dashboard can upload, view, and download SKU data.

**Can I manually tag products as HSA/FSA eligible in Shopify?**
No. Product eligibility must be verified and assigned by Truemed's compliance team. Shopify tagging alone does not enable HSA/FSA checkout.

**How long does review take?**
Typically 1–2 business days for standard reviews. Pre-approved products with missing GTINs may take an additional 1–2 days.

**What if I have thousands of products?**
The upload system handles large catalogs. If you run into issues with very large files, contact [merchants@truemed.com](mailto:merchants@truemed.com).
