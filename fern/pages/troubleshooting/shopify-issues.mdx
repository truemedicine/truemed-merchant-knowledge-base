## Payment Option Not Showing at Checkout

If your customers can't see the Truemed payment option at checkout, check the following:

### 1. Confirm the App Is Activated

1. Go to **Shopify Admin > Settings > Payments**
2. Look for "Truemed" under **Additional payment methods**
3. If it's not listed, you may need to reinstall or activate it. See the [Shopify Setup Guide](/implementation/shopify/setup-guide) article.

### 2. Check That Products Have Approved SKUs

If a product hasn't been reviewed by Truemed's compliance team, it will default to **ineligible** at checkout. Upload your latest product catalog through the Truemed Dashboard.

See the [SKU Upload](/troubleshooting/sku-upload) troubleshooting article for details.

### 3. Ensure the Customer Is Checking Out as Guest

Truemed's payment option does not appear when customers use **Shop Pay** or accelerated checkout. Customers must select **"Guest checkout"** to see Truemed as a payment method.

### 4. Verify Stripe Account Status

Your Stripe account must be at least **Enabled** for the payment app to work. Check your Stripe connection status in your onboarding plan or contact [merchants@truemed.com](mailto:merchants@truemed.com).

### 5. Check Geographic Restrictions

Truemed is currently available to US customers only. If a customer's billing or shipping address is outside the US, the Truemed option may not appear.

## Missing Orders in Shopify

Truemed orders are processed through our payment system, not through Shopify's standard payment flow. Here's what to know:

- Truemed orders **do appear in Shopify** but may behave differently from standard orders
- Orders start in a **"Pending" state** until a Letter of Medical Necessity (LMN) is issued
- Status transitions can take a **few hours**, and this is normal
- Check the [Truemed Dashboard](https://app.truemed.com) for the most up-to-date order status

### Order Status Reference

**Shopify Statuses:**

| Shopify Status | Meaning | Action Required |
|----------------|---------|-----------------|
| **Payment Pending** | Health survey is under review. No LMN issued yet. | Wait. Do not fulfill. |
| **Authorized** | LMN has been issued. Payment can be captured. | Capture payment within 5 days. |
| **Paid** | Payment has been captured. | Fulfill the order. |
| **Expired** | LMN was rejected or authorization expired. | Do not fulfill. |

**Truemed Dashboard Statuses:**

| Truemed Dashboard Status | Meaning |
|--------------------------|---------|
| **Processing** | Health survey under review |
| **Authorized** | LMN issued. Eligible for capture and fulfillment |
| **Rejected** | LMN rejected. Order cancelled |
| **Captured** | Payment successfully captured |

## Manual Capture

### What Is Manual Capture?

Manual capture is a Shopify setting that allows you to **authorize** a charge but delay the **capture** (collection) of funds until later. Some merchants use this for preorders or custom fulfillment workflows.

Truemed is optimized for **automatic capture**, which is Shopify's default.

### How to Check Your Capture Setting

1. Go to **Shopify Admin > Settings > Payments**
2. Look for **Payment capture method**
3. If set to **"Automatically capture payment for orders"**, you're all set. No action needed.

### If You Use Manual Capture

Manual capture works with Truemed, but requires special attention because Truemed orders have unique status transitions:

#### The Manual Capture Flow

1. **Order created:** Customer places order through Truemed. Payment is authorized but not captured.
2. **LMN review:** The order remains in "Pending" state while the health survey is reviewed.
3. **LMN issued:** Order transitions to "Authorized" status (this can take several hours).
4. **Capture window:** You must capture payment **within 5 days** of authorization, or it expires.
5. **Payment captured:** Order moves to "Paid" and is ready to fulfill.

#### How to Capture Payment

- **Bulk capture:** From the Shopify Orders list, select eligible orders and use bulk actions
- **Individual capture:** Open the order and click **"Capture payment"**

<Note>
You can only capture payment **once per order**. Partial captures are not supported.
</Note>

#### Common Manual Capture Issues

| Issue | Cause | Solution |
|-------|-------|----------|
| **Missed captures** | Automation ran before the order transitioned to "Authorized" | Add retry logic to check for delayed status changes |
| **Expired authorization** | Payment not captured within 5 days | Customer must re-place the order. Expired authorizations cannot be recovered. |
| **False fraud flags** | Fraud detection ran before order reached "Paid" status | Wait for correct status before fraud evaluation |

#### Recommended: Shopify Flow for Auto-Capture

If you want to use manual capture but still auto-capture Truemed orders, create a dedicated Shopify Flow:

1. **Trigger:** Order payment status changes
2. **Condition:** Payment gateway contains "Truemed" AND order is "Authorized"
3. **Action:** Capture payment

<Tip>
A downloadable example Shopify Flow template is available. Reach out to [merchants@truemed.com](mailto:merchants@truemed.com) to request it. Test thoroughly in your specific environment before going live.
</Tip>

## Theme Changes

**Will changing my Shopify theme affect Truemed?**

- **Payment app:** No. The Truemed payment integration is independent of your theme.
- **Product page widget:** Yes. If you switch themes, you'll need to re-add the PDP widget to your new theme template. See the [Shopify Setup Guide](/implementation/shopify/setup-guide) article for installation instructions.
- **Thank You page widget:** If using the Truemed Extensions app (Checkout Extensibility), no action is needed. If using Additional Scripts, verify your scripts are still in place after the theme change.

## Refunds for Truemed Orders

Refunds for Truemed orders are processed through the Truemed Dashboard, not through Shopify's standard refund flow:

- Refund amounts are deducted from your Stripe payout balance
- Refunds are initiated from the Truemed Dashboard or by contacting [merchants@truemed.com](mailto:merchants@truemed.com)
- Processing time follows standard Stripe timelines

## Quick Troubleshooting Checklist

| Symptom | First Thing to Check |
|---------|---------------------|
| Truemed not showing at checkout | App activated? Stripe enabled? Customer using Guest checkout? |
| Order stuck in "Pending" | LMN review in progress. Check Truemed Dashboard for status |
| Can't capture payment | Is the order "Authorized"? Has the 5-day window expired? |
| Product showing as ineligible | Upload latest SKU list via Truemed Dashboard |
| Widget not appearing on product page | Check theme template. Widget may need to be re-added after a theme change |
