## **Launch Email Copy (Subscription Purchases)**

> Exciting news — we’ve partnered with Truemed to make it easier for qualified customers to use Health Savings Account (HSA) or Flexible Spending Account (FSA) funds toward eligible \[Brand Name\] products.
>
> That means if you qualify, you can purchase products using pre-tax dollars — resulting in average savings of around 30%.\*
>
> HSAs and FSAs were created to help you spend pre-tax dollars on products and services that may help treat or prevent specific medical conditions. Truemed helps make that process simple:
>
> **1. Make your purchase:**
> Browse our selection and add your go-to products or new finds to your cart. Do not pay with an HSA/FSA card during checkout. After your purchase, check your confirmation screen or receipt email for a link to the Truemed survey.
>
> **2. Complete health assessment:**
> Take a private health survey that you can find in your order confirmation. A licensed provider will review your answers to determine eligibility. If eligible, you'll receive a Letter of Medical Necessity (LMN) within 12 hours. Note: The survey must be taken on the same day as your purchase.
>
> **3. Submit for HSA/FSA Reimbursement:**
> Follow the guide included with your LMN to submit your purchase for reimbursement. Claims are usually paid out in 2-4 weeks.
>
> Truemed’s mission is to make true medicine more affordable and accessible. Learn more at [truemed.com](http://truemed.com).
>
> \*Truemed is for qualified customers. Average savings are based on pre-tax benefit use and vary by individual. Learn more at truemed.com/disclosures.

It also preserves trust and lowers the chance of disputes or chargebacks by setting accurate expectations about eligibility and savings.
