### **How to Implement HSA/FSA in Extension Ads**

- Add an HSA/FSA snippet to your Google Ads structured snippets extension to ensure that users immediately see potential savings when viewing the ad.

- Example: "HSA/FSA Eligible"

2\. **Callout Extensions**

- Utilize callout extensions to reinforce the message.

- Example: "Your purchase may be HSA/FSA Eligible. Save an average of 30% if qualified."

- This appears alongside your main ad copy, increasing visibility.

3\. **Sitelink Extensions for SEO Control**

- Sitelinks allow for deeper engagement and can be controlled for SEO optimization.

- Ensure that one of the indented sitelinks directs users to a [**landing page**](https://support.truemed.com/en/articles/4657729) dedicated to HSA/FSA eligibility.

4\. **Price Extensions**

- Display specific products or categories that qualify for HSA/FSA payments.

- Example: "Eligible Supplements – Starting at $19.99 – Pay with HSA/FSA if Qualified"

### **Best Practices**

- **Ensure Accuracy:** Double-check that all advertised products meet HSA/FSA eligibility requirements.

- **Dedicated Landing Page:** Link sitelinks to a clear, [**informative page**](https://support.truemed.com/en/articles/4657729) about HSA/FSA payments.
