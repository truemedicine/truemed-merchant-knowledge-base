Use these social-ready templates to inform, educate, and convert with your new HSA/FSA payment option[.](http://option.in/) In this guide, you’ll find approved captions, image prompts, and posting tips that align with Truemed’s compliance standards—including how to use phrases like “save an average of 30% with HSA/FSA” when paired with our required disclaimer.

- \[Product Name\] is HSA/FSA Eligible

- Pay with HSA/FSA at checkout

- You may be able to use HSA/FSA for \[Product Name\]

- Qualified customers can use HSA/FSA for \[Product Name\]

- Save an average of 30% with HSA/FSA (if eligible)

- Eligible customers save an average of 30% with HSA/FSA

- Your purchase from \[Brand Name\] may be HSA/FSA eligible

**Important:** Please include our compliance disclaimer **visibly** on any & all HSA/FSA messaging:

> Truemed is for qualified customers. HSA/FSA tax savings vary. Learn more at [truemed.com/disclosures](http://truemed.com/disclosures)

If the disclaimer is missing, we may ask you to update or remove the asset.

Tag us (@truemedpayments) in your post or feel free to invite us to collaborate. Our social team will review posts as they come in, and would be happy to collaborate when we are able.

> Truemed is for qualified customers. HSA/FSA tax savings vary. Learn more at [truemed.com/disclosures](http://truemed.com/disclosures)

### **What NOT To Say:**

- Save up to 30% (This is incorrect. Eligible customers save an *average* of 30%)

- Now HSA/FSA Eligible (You were always HSA/FSA Eligible, Truemed just makes it possible to accept these funds directly)

- Indicate that all customers are automatically eligible.

Note: If our team is made aware of any noncompliant copy, we will reach out to ask you to update or remove the asset.

Compliance Reminder**

It also preserves trust and lowers the chance of disputes or chargebacks by setting accurate expectations about eligibility and savings.
