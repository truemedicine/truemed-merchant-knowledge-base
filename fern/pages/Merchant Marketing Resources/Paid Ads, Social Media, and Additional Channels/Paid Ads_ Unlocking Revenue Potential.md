## **Targeting Strategies for HSA/FSA Consumers**

- **Health Interests:** Target users interested in health, wellness, fitness, or specific health-related topics like biohacking, longevity, or wellness tech.

- **Occupation and Industry:** Focus on professions likely to have employer-sponsored HSA/FSA benefits, such as healthcare, tech, or corporate roles.

- **Income Brackets:** Use demographic targeting for higher income brackets, as these individuals are more likely to have access to HSA/FSA plans.

- **Life Events:** Target life stages like "new parents" or "recently married," as they may be managing healthcare costs more actively.

- **Behavioral Signals:** Look for users who engage with health and fitness apps, make online health-related purchases, or show interest in tax benefits.
