---
title: "Installing the Truemed Payment App"
---

The Truemed app is not listed on Shopify, so you will need to either receive a link to the app from an engineer or use the link already provided on your onboarding document. All of this happens live during your onboarding call - you’ll install the app and set up your Truemed account in just a few clicks.

**How you will receive payouts:** 

Payouts for orders placed through Truemed are processed via Stripe and will be deposited directly into the bank account you connected. While customers complete their purchase through the Truemed checkout option in Shopify, we handle the transaction on your behalf—so the order does not process through your Shopify store, but you still receive the payout seamlessly.
