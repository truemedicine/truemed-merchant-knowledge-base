---
title: "We sell in the US but are not a US-based company. How can I onboard with Stripe_"
---

You will need an EIN number, a US phone number, and a US address to complete our Stripe onboarding. You can apply as a foreign entity for an EIN number - [here](https://www.irs.gov/businesses/small-businesses-self-employed/how-to-apply-for-an-ein#:~:text=Apply%20by%20telephone%20%2D%20International%20applicants) are the instructions to do so. If you do not have a US phone number affiliated with your business, you can use the resources [here](https://www.openphone.com/blog/how-to-get-us-phone-number/) to get one, and you should use a US address affiliated with your business.
