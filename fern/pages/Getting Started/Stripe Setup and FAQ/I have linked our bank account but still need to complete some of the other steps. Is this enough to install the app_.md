---
title: "I have linked our bank account but still need to complete some of the other steps. Is this enough to install the app_"
---

Although we only use Stripe to send payments to that linked bank account, Stripe prevents us from paying out until your account is “Complete” or “Enabled”. As long as your account’s status is “Restricted” you will not be able to receive payouts, so we cannot install the Truemed payment app.
