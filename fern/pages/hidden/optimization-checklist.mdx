---
title: 'Optimization Checklist'
---

This checklist outlines the exact strategies top Truemed performers use to maximize revenue. From site updates to email campaigns and ads, these proven steps make it simple to highlight HSA/FSA eligibility and capture more year-round sales.

---

![](https://cdn.applied.guide/media/886214b8-01bd-4c9d-8591-1d6ccbcc8c0e/31a4ee45-44b3-452e-a2b5-3e6fb3d4db8b.png)## **Website Optimization:**

☐ **Create a High-Converting Landing Page**

- Use our[ **Landing Page Guide**](https://support.truemed.com/resources/hsa-fsa-landing-page) to design a page that drives conversions.

- Access our[ **Figma Template**](https://support.truemed.com/resources/hsa-fsa-landing-page#figma_file_templates) to simplify the creation process.

☐ **Ensure the** [**PDP Widget**](https://support.truemed.com/resources/product-page-widget-2) **and** [**Truemed Payment App**](https://support.truemed.com/resources/checkout-integration) **are Installed**

☐ **Add a** [**Banner**](https://support.truemed.com/resources/homepage-banner) **to your Homepage announcing HSA/FSA eligibility**

☐ [**Update FAQs**](https://support.truemed.com/resources/faq-page-updates) **on your site to answer questions about HSA/FSA eligibility**

![](https://cdn.applied.guide/media/886214b8-01bd-4c9d-8591-1d6ccbcc8c0e/dedd1afd-fc08-4242-aad9-532831246688.png)## **Email Promotion**

☐ **Send a** [**Launch Email**](https://support.truemed.com/resources/launch-email) **announcing the partnership**

☐ **Send monthly or quarterly** [**Steady State Emails**](https://support.truemed.com/resources/steady-state-emails#steady_state_email) **to remind customers of HSA/FSA payment**

☐ **Add an** [**"HSA/FSA Eligible" Footer**](https://support.truemed.com/resources/steady-state-emails#email_footer) **to all emails**

☐ **Ensure year-round HSA/FSA messaging:**

- Make sure your marketing team incorporates these [**seasonal strategie**s](https://support.truemed.com/resources/the-q4-opportunity-and-optimization-checklist) into HSA/FSA-focused email campaigns throughout the year(especially in Q4, when the overwhelming majority of HSA/FSA funds are spent!)

- In the [**Seasonal Marketing Guide**](https://support.truemed.com/en/categories/1091777), you'll find templates, sample messaging, and strategy to add to your marketing calendar.

![](https://cdn.applied.guide/media/886214b8-01bd-4c9d-8591-1d6ccbcc8c0e/ac8c9de3-d589-4bef-b11a-6625f7d24b68.png)## **Social & Paid Ad Promotion**

☐ **Test Paid Ads Featuring HSA/FSA Spend**

- Follow our[ Paid Ads Guide](https://support.truemed.com/resources/paid-ads-unlocking-revenue-potential) to create high-converting campaigns (and find our [seasonal ad templates here](https://support.truemed.com/resources/hsa-fsa-ad-templates-for-bfcm-and-year-end-promotions)).

- Optimize ad creatives to highlight the benefits of HSA/FSA spending.

☐ **Share [social posts](https://support.truemed.com/resources/social-channels) announcing & reminding your customers of HSA/FSA payment.**
