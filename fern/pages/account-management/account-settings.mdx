This guide covers how to update key account settings across your Truemed Dashboard and Truemed-linked Stripe account. Whether you need to update business details, payout information, payment method, or membership, you'll find step-by-step instructions for each below.

***

## Updating Your Business Details

Business information like industry or EIN is managed in your Truemed-linked Stripe account, not the Truemed Dashboard.

1. Log in to your Truemed-linked Stripe account
2. Click the profile icon in the upper right
3. Select **Platform Settings**
4. Under **Business Details**, update fields such as your industry or EIN

***

## Updating Your Linked Bank Account

If you need to change the bank account receiving your Truemed payouts, update it directly in your Truemed-linked Stripe account.

1. Log in to your Truemed-linked Stripe account
2. Click the profile icon in the upper right
3. Select **Payout Details**
4. Click the edit icon (pen and paper) next to your current bank account
5. Enter your new bank account details and save

<Note>
Only users with **admin access** to your Stripe account can update payout information.
</Note>

***

## Updating Your Card on File

To update the payment method associated with your Truemed account:

1. Sign in to [app.truemed.com](https://app.truemed.com)
2. Select **Settings** in the top right corner
3. Click **Payment Method**
4. Update your payment method as needed
