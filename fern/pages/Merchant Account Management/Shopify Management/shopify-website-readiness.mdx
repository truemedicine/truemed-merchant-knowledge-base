Your Truemed payment app is installed and active — now let's make sure your Shopify store is set up to educate customers about HSA/FSA eligibility and maximize conversions. This guide walks you through the on-site elements that drive HSA/FSA awareness before, during, and after checkout.

<Callout type="info">
  Complete the [Shopify Setup Guide](/merchant-account-management/shopify-management/shopify-setup-guide) before starting this page. You'll need your **Public Qualification ID** for several steps below. If you don't have it, reach out to [merchants@truemed.com](mailto:merchants@truemed.com).
</Callout>

## Widget placement

The product page widget is a small eligibility message that appears on your product pages, typically near the price or "Add to Cart" button. It tells customers that their purchase may qualify for HSA/FSA spending or reimbursement — and it's one of the highest-impact elements for driving HSA/FSA conversions.

### Installing the widget

<Steps>
  <Step title="Find your Public Qualification ID">
    Your Public Qualification ID was provided during onboarding and is visible in your [Truemed Dashboard](https://app.truemed.com). If you can't find it, email [merchants@truemed.com](mailto:merchants@truemed.com).
  </Step>

  <Step title="Open your theme editor">
    In your Shopify admin, go to **Online Store > Themes**. On your active theme, click **Customize**. Navigate to your **Default product template**.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Add a custom code block">
    In the left-hand editor panel, find the section near your **price** or **Buy / Add to Cart** buttons. Click **Add block** and select **Custom Liquid** (or **Custom HTML** depending on your theme).
  </Step>

  <Step title="Paste the widget code">
    Copy the code below and paste it into the custom block. Replace \`YOUR_PUBLIC_QUALIFICATION_ID\` with your actual ID.

    \`\`\`html title="Product Page Widget"
    <div id="truemed-instructions" style="font-size: 14px;" icon-height="12" data-public-id="YOUR_PUBLIC_QUALIFICATION_ID"></div>
    <script src="https://static.truemed.com/widgets/product-page-widget.min.js" defer></script>
    \`\`\`

    <Callout type="tip">
      You can adjust the \`style\` attribute to match your store's design.
    </Callout>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Save and preview">
    Click **Save** in the theme editor, then preview a product page on your live store to confirm the widget is displaying correctly.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Dark mode support

If your product pages have a dark background, add the `dark-mode` attribute to enable a white Truemed logo and adjust text colors:

```html title="Widget with dark mode"
<div dark-mode id="truemed-instructions" style="font-size: 14px; color: #ffffff;" icon-height="12" data-public-id="YOUR_PUBLIC_QUALIFICATION_ID"></div>
<script src="https://static.truemed.com/widgets/product-page-widget.min.js" defer></script>
```

### Display on specific products only

If your store sells a mix of eligible and ineligible products (for example, you also sell gift cards or branded merch), you can control which products show the widget using Shopify tags.

<Tabs>
  <Tab label="Show only eligible products">
    First, add the tag `truemed-eligible` to your eligible products in Shopify (under **Products > [Product] > Tags**).

    Then use this version of the widget code:

    ```html title="Display if tagged eligible"
    <div shopify-tags="display-if-eligible" id="truemed-instructions" style="font-size: 14px;" icon-height="12" data-public-id="YOUR_PUBLIC_QUALIFICATION_ID"></div>
    <script src="https://static.truemed.com/widgets/product-page-widget.min.js" defer></script>
    ```

    Best for stores where **fewer products are eligible** than ineligible.
  </Tab>

  <Tab label="Hide ineligible products">
    Add the tag `truemed-ineligible` to products that should not show the widget (like gift cards).

    Then use this version:

    ```html title="Hide if tagged ineligible"
    <div shopify-tags="display-unless-ineligible" id="truemed-instructions" style="font-size: 14px;" icon-height="12" data-public-id="YOUR_PUBLIC_QUALIFICATION_ID"></div>
    <script src="https://static.truemed.com/widgets/product-page-widget.min.js" defer></script>
    ```

    Best for stores where **most products are eligible** but a few are not.
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

### Common styling adjustments

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion id="style-link-color">
    <summary>Change the "Learn how" link color</summary>

    Add this CSS to your theme or directly above the widget code:

    ```html
    <style>
      .truemed-instructions-open {
        color: #7a7a7a !important;
      }
    </style>
    ```
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion id="style-logo-size">
    <summary>Adjust the Truemed logo size</summary>

    ```html
    <style>
      .truemed-logo-img {
        height: 13px !important;
      }
    </style>
    ```
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion id="style-logo-alignment">
    <summary>Align the logo inline with text</summary>

    ```html
    <style>
      .truemed-logo-img {
        margin: 2px 0 0 3px !important;
      }
    </style>
    ```
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion id="style-spacing">
    <summary>Add spacing above or below the widget</summary>

    Add `margin-top` or `margin-bottom` to the widget's `style` attribute:

    ```html
    <div id="truemed-instructions" style="font-size: 14px; margin-top: 12px; margin-bottom: 12px;" icon-height="12" data-public-id="YOUR_PUBLIC_QUALIFICATION_ID"></div>
    ```
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

### Alternative: Edit theme code directly

If you prefer to work directly in your theme files (or your development team handles theme changes), you can add the widget code to your product template file:

1. In Shopify admin, go to **Online Store > Themes > Actions > Edit Code**
2. Find your product template — usually named something like `main-product.liquid` or `product.json`
3. Paste the widget code near the price or buy button section
4. Save

<Callout type="tip">
  This widget works for both Payment Integration and Reimbursement integration types. Based on your Public Qualification ID, Truemed automatically loads the correct version for your merchant profile.
</Callout>

## Checkout flow

Beyond the product page widget, there are two post-checkout elements that improve the customer experience and drive reimbursement completion rates.

### Order confirmation page (Thank You page)

The order confirmation widget appears on the Shopify Thank You page after a customer completes checkout. It guides customers on next steps — whether that's confirming their LMN was issued or starting the reimbursement process.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open your theme editor">
    Go to **Online Store > Themes > Customize** on your active theme. Navigate to the **Checkout and thank you page** template (or **Order status** page, depending on your Shopify plan).
  </Step>

  <Step title="Add the Truemed block">
    Click **Add block** or **Add section** and insert a **Custom Liquid** block. Paste the order confirmation widget code provided by your Truemed onboarding contact.

    <Callout type="info">
      The order confirmation code is unique to your account and includes your Qualification ID. You'll find it in your onboarding plan or by contacting [merchants@truemed.com](mailto:merchants@truemed.com).
    </Callout>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Save and verify">
    Save your changes. Place a test order to confirm the widget appears correctly on the Thank You page.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Order confirmation email snippet

In addition to the on-page widget, you can add a Truemed snippet to the order confirmation **email** that Shopify sends to customers. This reinforces the HSA/FSA message in their inbox.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open notification settings">
    In Shopify admin, go to **Settings > Notifications > Customer notifications > Order confirmation**.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Edit the email template">
    Click **Edit code** on the Order confirmation notification. Search for the order summary section in the template.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Paste the Truemed snippet">
    Insert the Truemed code snippet provided by your onboarding contact just below the order summary section. Save the template.

    <Callout type="warning">
      Be careful when editing notification templates — a misplaced tag can break the email layout. If you're unsure, send the code to your development team or contact [merchants@truemed.com](mailto:merchants@truemed.com) for help.
    </Callout>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Send a test email">
    Use Shopify's **Send test notification** feature to preview the email and confirm the snippet renders correctly.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Theme check

After completing your setup, run through this checklist to catch common issues before customers see them.

### Quick verification checklist

| Check | Where to look | What you're looking for |
|---|---|---|
| Payment option visible at checkout | Place a test order and proceed to payment | "Truemed" or "Pay with HSA/FSA" appears as a payment option |
| Product page widget displaying | Visit a product page on your live store | HSA/FSA eligibility message near the price or Add to Cart button |
| Widget shows on mobile | Open a product page on your phone or use Chrome DevTools | Widget is visible and properly formatted, not overlapping other elements |
| Order confirmation widget | Complete a test checkout and view the Thank You page | Truemed next-steps message appears after purchase |
| Order confirmation email | Check the test email sent by Shopify | Truemed snippet appears in the order confirmation email body |
| Dark mode (if applicable) | View a product page with a dark background | Truemed logo appears in white, text is readable |
| Tag filtering (if applicable) | Check an ineligible product page | Widget should NOT appear on products without the relevant tag |

<Callout type="tip">
  If the Truemed payment option doesn't appear at checkout, double-check that the app is **activated** (not just installed). Go to **Settings > Payments > Additional payment methods > Truemed** and verify the status shows as active.
</Callout>

### Common issues and fixes

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion id="fix-no-payment-option">
    <summary>Truemed payment option doesn't appear at checkout</summary>

    **Most likely cause:** The app is installed but not activated.

    **Fix:** Go to **Settings > Payments > Additional payment methods > Truemed** and click **Activate**.

    If it's already active, check that your Stripe account is fully connected. An "Enabled" status in Stripe means the account exists but may still need verification. A "Complete" status means it's fully ready. Contact [merchants@truemed.com](mailto:merchants@truemed.com) if your Stripe status isn't showing "Complete."
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion id="fix-widget-missing">
    <summary>Product page widget disappeared after a theme update</summary>

    **Cause:** The widget code lives inside your theme. A new theme won't include it automatically.

    **Fix:** Re-add the widget code to your new theme by following the [Widget placement](#widget-placement) steps above. Payments and order processing are unaffected — only the on-page widget needs to be re-installed.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion id="fix-widget-overlap">
    <summary>Widget overlaps with other elements on mobile</summary>

    **Fix:** Add `margin-top` and `margin-bottom` to the widget's style attribute to create spacing. You can also adjust `font-size` for smaller screens. Test across several breakpoints (phone, tablet, desktop) after making changes.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion id="fix-wrong-products">
    <summary>Widget shows on products that aren't HSA/FSA eligible</summary>

    **Fix:** Use Shopify tag filtering. Tag ineligible products with `truemed-ineligible` and add the `shopify-tags="display-unless-ineligible"` attribute to your widget code. See [Display on specific products only](#display-on-specific-products-only) above.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Test transaction

Before announcing HSA/FSA payments to your customers, run a complete test to make sure everything works end to end.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Visit a product page">
    Confirm the product page widget is visible and the "Learn how" link opens the Truemed information modal.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Add to cart and proceed to checkout">
    Add an eligible product to your cart and proceed to checkout. On the payment step, confirm that Truemed (or "Pay with HSA/FSA") appears as a payment option.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Complete a test purchase">
    Select the Truemed payment option and complete the health survey flow. This will generate a test order in both your Shopify admin and your Truemed Dashboard.

    <Callout type="info">
      For a test transaction, you can use your own information. The health survey is a standard part of the Truemed checkout experience and takes about 60 seconds for the customer.
    </Callout>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Verify the Thank You page">
    After completing the purchase, check the order confirmation page for the Truemed widget. It should display next steps relevant to the customer's payment type (HSA/FSA card or credit card + reimbursement).
  </Step>

  <Step title="Check your Shopify orders">
    In your Shopify admin, go to **Orders** and find the test order. Confirm it appears correctly. If you're using auto-capture (the default), the payment status should transition from "Pending" to "Paid" within a few hours as the LMN is processed.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Verify in your Truemed Dashboard">
    Log into your [Truemed Dashboard](https://app.truemed.com) and confirm the test order appears under **Orders**. This confirms the full integration is working.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="tip">
  After your test is successful, refund the test order from your Shopify admin to avoid any charges. Truemed refunds process through Stripe just like standard Shopify refunds.
</Callout>

<Accordion id="faqs">
  <summary>Frequently asked questions</summary>

  **Does this widget work with all Shopify themes?**

  Yes. The widget uses a standard HTML/JavaScript snippet that works with any Shopify theme. You can add it through the visual theme editor (Custom Liquid block) or by editing the theme code directly.

  **Will Truemed work with Shopify Plus checkout extensibility?**

  Yes. If you're on Shopify Plus with checkout extensibility enabled, reach out to [merchants@truemed.com](mailto:merchants@truemed.com) for the Plus-specific configuration guide.

  **Does the widget work for both payment and reimbursement integrations?**

  Yes. Based on your Public Qualification ID, the widget automatically loads the version tailored to your merchant profile — whether you're set up for direct HSA/FSA payment, post-purchase reimbursement, or both (hybrid).

  **Can I install the widget on only some product pages?**

  Yes. Use Shopify product tags (`truemed-eligible` or `truemed-ineligible`) and the corresponding widget attribute. See [Display on specific products only](#display-on-specific-products-only) for details.

  **What happens to existing Truemed orders if I change my theme?**

  Nothing — existing orders, payment processing, and payouts are completely unaffected. Only the product page widget (which is embedded in the theme code) needs to be re-added to a new theme.
</Accordion>
