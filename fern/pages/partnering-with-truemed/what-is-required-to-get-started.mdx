Before you can start accepting HSA/FSA payments through Truemed, you'll need a few things in place. This page covers onboarding requirements, Stripe setup, and the technical prerequisites for each supported platform.

## Onboarding requirements

To partner with Truemed, your business must:

- **Sell to U.S. customers.** Truemed is currently available in the United States only. Your business does not need to be U.S.-headquartered (see international merchant notes below).
- **Offer products that can be medically substantiated.** Truemed works with products and services that treat, mitigate, or prevent a diagnosed medical condition under IRS guidelines. Truemed evaluates product eligibility during the partnership process.
- **Complete Stripe onboarding.** Truemed uses Stripe to process payments and issue payouts. You'll set up a Stripe account on the Truemed platform during onboarding.
- **Have a team member with admin access available for setup.** Depending on your platform, you'll need someone who can install apps or adjust payment settings during a live onboarding call.

Most implementations take 30 minutes or less.

## Stripe account

Truemed uses Stripe to collect funds from your customers' HSA/FSA cards and transfer them to your linked bank account.

**What to know:**

- You'll create a new Stripe account under the Truemed platform, even if you already have an existing Stripe account. You can use your existing credentials to log in.
- You must connect a valid bank account so Truemed can remit payouts. This connection is encrypted and managed through Stripe. Truemed does not store your banking credentials.
- Payouts typically settle within 48 hours on a rolling basis.
- After onboarding, your Stripe account will show as either "Enabled" or "Complete." Both statuses can receive payouts. Stripe may request additional information over time based on account volume and age.

**International merchants:** You'll need an EIN, a U.S. phone number, and a U.S. business address to complete Stripe onboarding. You can [apply for an EIN as a foreign entity](https://www.irs.gov/businesses/small-businesses-self-employed/get-an-employer-identification-number) through the IRS.

## Technical requirements by platform

### Shopify

- At least one team member with account-level permissions to install and manage third-party payment apps.
- The Truemed app is installed via a direct link during your onboarding call (it is not listed on the Shopify App Store).

### WooCommerce

- Admin access to install and configure the Truemed plugin.
- Setup details are provided during your onboarding call.

### BigCommerce

- Admin access to your BigCommerce storefront.
- Integration steps are provided during your onboarding call.

### Custom API

- Your development team integrates with Truemed's API endpoints for checkout, authorization, and capture.
- Full developer documentation is available at [developers.truemed.com](https://developers.truemed.com).

### Reimbursements (post-purchase)

- No app installation required. Works with any e-commerce platform or in-store environment.
- You'll embed a widget or link on your order confirmation page, or include a link in your post-purchase email flow.
- Your unique qualification survey link is available in the [Truemed dashboard](https://app.truemed.com).
