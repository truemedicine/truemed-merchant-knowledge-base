This guide walks you through setting up Truemed's post-purchase reimbursement flow. This integration is for merchants who sell subscription products, don't have a Shopify storefront, or want to offer HSA/FSA reimbursement alongside (or instead of) direct HSA/FSA card payments.

---

## How the Reimbursement Flow Works

Instead of paying directly with an HSA/FSA card at checkout, your customers:

1. **Purchase normally** using any payment method on your site
2. **Complete a health survey** via a Truemed qualification link (shown post-purchase)
3. **Receive an LMN:** If eligible, a licensed provider issues a Letter of Medical Necessity within 24-48 hours.
4. **Submit for reimbursement:** The customer submits the LMN and receipt to their HSA/FSA administrator for reimbursement.

Each LMN covers the customer's eligible purchases for a **full year**, so they only need to qualify once annually.

## Prerequisites

Before you begin setup:

- ☐ You've created your Truemed account at [app.truemed.com](https://app.truemed.com)
- ☐ You've uploaded your product catalog (CSV with Name, SKU, and Description columns)
- ☐ Your products have been reviewed and categorized by Truemed's compliance team

---

## Step 1: Create Your Truemed Account

If you haven't already, create your Truemed account using the link provided by your Truemed contact. This gives you access to the Truemed Dashboard, where you can:

- View **Qualifications** to see customer qualification activity and LMN status
- Manage **Settings** to add team members and update your payment method
- Upload **Products** to submit new products for eligibility review

---

## Step 2: Provide Payment for LMN Fees

Truemed charges a flat fee for each LMN issued through your qualification link. Fees are collected in batches every Thursday.

1. Go to [app.truemed.com/merchants/qualifications](https://app.truemed.com/merchants/qualifications)
2. Add a payment method (credit card)
3. Once saved, your **Qualification Link** will appear on this page

<Warning>
You are charged each time an LMN is issued. Do not place the qualification link on pre-purchase pages (landing pages, marketing emails, etc.). It should only appear **after** a customer has completed a purchase.
</Warning>

---

## Step 3: Add the Qualification Link to Your Thank You Page

This is the most important touchpoint. Showing the qualification link immediately after purchase gives customers the smoothest path to reimbursement.

### Shopify Stores: Checkout Extensibility (Recommended)

If your Shopify store uses Checkout Extensibility:

1. Visit [apps.shopify.com/truemed-extensions](https://apps.shopify.com/truemed-extensions) and click **Install**
2. Follow the configuration prompts
3. Enter your **Truemed Public ID** (the last segment of your Qualification Link)
4. Save your changes

<Tip>
To show the qualification link only for subscription orders, toggle this setting within the checkout extension editor.
</Tip>

### Shopify Stores: Additional Scripts (Legacy)

If you haven't migrated to Checkout Extensibility:

1. Find your Qualification Link at [app.truemed.com](https://app.truemed.com)
2. In Shopify, go to **Settings > Checkout > Additional Scripts**
3. Paste in the "Order status page" section:

```html
<div id="truemed-reimburse" data-url="YOUR_QUALIFICATION_LINK"></div>
<script src="https://truemed-public.s3.us-west-1.amazonaws.com/truemed-ads/confirmation-widget-v1.1.min.js"></script>
```

### Non-Shopify Stores

Add a CTA on your post-purchase/order confirmation page:

```html
<p>This order might be eligible for HSA/FSA reimbursement. <a href="YOUR_QUALIFICATION_LINK?source=post_purchase">Get Reimbursed</a>.</p>
```

## Step 4: Add the Qualification Link to Your Order Confirmation Email

This acts as a backup in case a customer misses the Thank You page. Add the link to your transactional order confirmation email template.

**Example snippet:**

```html
<p>This order might be eligible for HSA/FSA reimbursement. <a href="YOUR_QUALIFICATION_LINK?source=email_receipt">Get Reimbursed</a>.</p>
```

---

## Step 5: Install the Product Page Widget

The PDP widget informs customers about HSA/FSA eligibility directly on the product page. This is the highest-impact promotion you can implement.

1. Get your **Public Qualification ID** from [app.truemed.com/merchants/qualifications](https://app.truemed.com/merchants/qualifications)
2. Add this code near the "Add to Cart" or "Buy" button on your product pages:

```html
<div id="truemed-instructions" style="font-size: 14px;" icon-height="12" data-public-id="YOUR_PUBLIC_QUALIFICATION_ID"></div>
<script src="https://static.truemed.com/widgets/product-page-widget.min.js" defer></script>
```

The widget auto-detects your merchant profile and displays the appropriate messaging for the reimbursement flow.

## Step 6: Add Checkout Messaging for Subscriptions (Shopify, Optional)

If you sell subscriptions through Shopify, add a checkout banner so customers know about the reimbursement opportunity before completing their order.

1. Install [Checkout Blocks](https://apps.shopify.com/checkout-blocks)
2. Create a Dynamic Content Block with a Banner template
3. Set display rule: show when any line item has a selling plan
4. Use this approved copy:

> **Title:** Save an average of 30% when you use your HSA/FSA funds through Truemed.
>
> **Content:** Once you've purchased, fill out the Truemed health intake form on your order confirmation page to check if you qualify for HSA/FSA reimbursement. HSA/FSA tax savings vary. Learn more at [truemed.com/disclosures](https://www.truemed.com/disclosures).

5. Limit publishing region to **United States**
6. Save and add the block to your checkout

## Reimbursement Attribution (Optional but Recommended)

Track which customers are getting reimbursed and how they discover Truemed by adding query parameters to your qualification links.

### Source Parameter

Indicates where the customer clicked the link. Use a different value for each placement:

```
https://app.truemed.com/qualify/[QUALIFICATION_ID]?source=order_confirm
```

Common values: `email_receipt`, `order_confirm`, `email_payment_reminder`, `reactivate_campaign`

### User ID Parameter

For more granular user-level attribution:

```
https://app.truemed.com/qualify/[QUALIFICATION_ID]?source=order_confirm&user_id=customer@email.com
```

### SKU Parameter

To tailor the health survey to specific purchased items (contact [merchants@truemed.com](mailto:merchants@truemed.com) to enable):

```
https://app.truemed.com/qualify/[QUALIFICATION_ID]?skus=product123,product456
```

<Note>
All parameter values should be URL-encoded. Source and user_id values are reflected in the Truemed Dashboard and CSV reports.
</Note>
