Reduce friction at the point of purchase so qualified shoppers follow through. Adding HSA/FSA messaging to your product pages, cart, and checkout gives shoppers confidence their funds will be accepted before they click "buy."

Start with the PDP widget, then add cart and checkout badging.

## Install the PDP Widget

The Truemed widget appears on your product detail pages and tells qualified shoppers they may be able to pay with HSA/FSA funds. It sits alongside your price and "Add to Cart" button.

{/* IMAGE: Garmin's fēnix 8 Pro product page showing the Truemed widget below the price: "HSA/FSA eligible for qualified customers [Truemed logo]. Learn more" — positioned between the Affirm financing line and the Case Size selector. */}

Garmin places the widget directly below their price and financing options on the fēnix 8 Pro page. The copy reads "HSA/FSA eligible for qualified customers" with a Truemed logo and "Learn more" link that opens a modal explaining how to pay with HSA/FSA.

If you have an HSA/FSA landing page from the [Consideration](/consideration) stage, link the widget's "Learn more" to it.

### How to Implement

For Shopify merchants, the PDP widget install is covered in Step 3 of the [Shopify Setup Guide](/implementation/shopify/setup-guide). The install takes about 30 minutes and includes options for dark mode and showing the widget only on eligible products.

If you offer subscribe-and-save or any recurring purchase option, Steps 4 and 6 of the [Shopify Setup Guide](/implementation/shopify/setup-guide) cover the Thank You page widget and checkout banner for subscriptions.

## Add Cart and Checkout Badging

A shopper who saw the widget on the product page should see the HSA/FSA message again when reviewing their order. Consistent messaging through the funnel reduces drop-off.

### Item-Level vs. Summary-Level Badging

There are two approaches, and which one is right depends on your catalog.

**Item-level badging** places the HSA/FSA message on each eligible line item. This is the right approach for mixed-eligibility catalogs. Garmin does this well: in a cart containing a fēnix 8 Pro (eligible) and a TOPO US 24K West digital map (not eligible), only the watch shows "Eligible for HSA/FSA reimbursement with [Truemed]." The map has no badge.

{/* IMAGE: Garmin's cart page showing two items side by side. The fēnix 8 Pro displays "Eligible for HSA/FSA reimbursement with [Truemed logo]" below its price. The TOPO US 24K West download shows no badge. This is item-level badging for a mixed-eligibility catalog. */}

**Summary-level badging** places a single HSA/FSA message near the cart subtotal. This works for merchants whose entire catalog (or nearly all of it) is eligible.

<Warning>
For mixed-eligibility catalogs, always use item-level badging. A summary-level banner that implies the entire cart is eligible is non-compliant if some items do not qualify.
</Warning>

### Carry It Through to Checkout

The same badging should appear in the checkout order summary. Garmin carries their item-level badge into the checkout page: the fēnix 8 Pro still shows the Truemed badge in the order summary sidebar while the shopper enters their shipping and payment details.

{/* IMAGE: Garmin's checkout page showing the order summary sidebar. The fēnix 8 Pro line item displays "Eligible for HSA/FSA reimbursement with [Truemed logo]" below the price. The TOPO map line item shows no badge. */}

### Copy Patterns

Adjust the language to match your brand voice.

| Placement | Example Copy |
|---|---|
| Cart (item-level) | "Eligible for HSA/FSA reimbursement with Truemed." |
| Cart (summary-level) | "[Product category] purchases may be HSA/FSA eligible with Truemed." |
| Checkout (order summary) | "Eligible for HSA/FSA reimbursement with Truemed." |

Any copy that references savings must include the compliance disclaimer. A "Learn more" link to your landing page is acceptable in lieu of printing the full disclaimer in the cart drawer.

### How to Implement

Cart and checkout badging is available for Shopify merchants. Contact your CSM or [partners@truemed.com](mailto:partners@truemed.com) to turn it on.

## Compliance Reminder

All HSA/FSA messaging on product pages, cart, and checkout must include the following disclaimer:

*Truemed is for qualified customers. HSA/FSA tax savings vary. Learn more at truemed.com/disclosures.*

For the PDP widget, the disclaimer is handled within the widget or the "Learn more" modal. For cart and checkout badging, include it on the same page or link to your landing page.

<Warning>
Any messaging that references savings must include the compliance disclaimer. If the disclaimer is missing or copy is non-compliant, Truemed may ask you to update or remove the asset.
</Warning>
