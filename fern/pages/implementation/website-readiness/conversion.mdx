This is where site optimization translates into revenue. Merchants who add HSA/FSA messaging to their product pages, cart, and checkout see higher checkout conversion and lower cart abandonment because qualified shoppers have confidence their funds will be accepted before they click "buy."

This stage covers three things: the PDP widget, cart and checkout badging, and making sure your CX team is ready for the questions that follow.

## Install the PDP Widget

The Truemed widget appears on your product detail pages and tells qualified customers they may be able to pay with HSA/FSA funds. This is typically the first point in the purchase flow where the savings message appears.

Your onboarding call enabled Truemed's payment processing, but the widget still needs to be added to your storefront. This is typically a task for your developer or Shopify admin.

### Default vs. Customized Widget

The default widget works out of the box and displays a standard HSA/FSA eligibility message on the product page. A customized widget that matches your brand's design system and includes a "Learn more" link to your [HSA/FSA landing page](/marketing/website-optimization/consideration) tends to outperform the default. Talk to your CSM about customization options.

### Implementation

Send the [Widget Setup Guide](/integration-guides/widgets/pdp-widget) to your developer. The install takes about 30 minutes for the standard widget.

If you offer subscribe-and-save, auto-refill, or any recurring purchase option, you'll also need specific checkout messaging for subscription orders. See the [Subscription Messaging guide](/integration-guides/subscriptions/overview) and have your developer handle it alongside the widget install (about 20 additional minutes).

## Add Cart and Checkout Badging

Add HSA/FSA eligibility messaging in the cart and at the checkout payment step. This reinforces the savings message when the customer is deciding whether to complete their purchase.

### What Good Looks Like

**Good:** A text line in the cart summary stating the order may be eligible for HSA/FSA.
**Great:** A visual badge near the cart subtotal that links to your landing page. In checkout, a visual indicator at the payment method step highlighting Truemed as an option. For mixed catalogs, the badge only appears on eligible items, not as a cart-level banner.

### Copy Patterns

Use these as starting points. Adjust the language to match your brand voice while keeping claims factual and compliant.

| Placement | Example Copy |
|---|---|
| Cart (item-level) | "May be eligible for HSA/FSA with Truemed." |
| Cart (summary-level) | "[Product category] purchases may be HSA/FSA eligible. [Exclusions] excluded." |
| Checkout (payment step) | "Pay with HSA/FSA via Truemed. Qualified customers save an average of 30%.*" |

*Truemed is for qualified customers. HSA/FSA tax savings vary. Learn more at truemed.com/disclosures.*

<Warning>
Keep cart claims brief and factual. The required disclaimer must be accessible from any savings claim. A "Learn more" link to the landing page is acceptable in lieu of printing the full disclaimer in the cart drawer.
</Warning>

### Implementation

Cart and checkout badging typically requires a developer. Contact [merchants@truemed.com](mailto:merchants@truemed.com) for setup guidance specific to your cart drawer and checkout configuration.

## Brief Your CX Team

Before promoting HSA/FSA across your site and marketing channels, make sure your support team can handle the questions that follow. Share the [Compliant Messaging Guide](/getting-started/compliant-messaging) with them and confirm they can explain:

1. What HSA/FSA is and how it works with your store.
2. How the Truemed checkout process works (selecting Truemed, completing the clinical intake form, receiving the LMN).
3. What to do if a customer's qualification is not approved.

Common customer questions your team should be ready for:

| Question | How to Handle |
|---|---|
| "Why wasn't I approved?" | The determination is made by an independent licensed practitioner based on individual health circumstances. Truemed cannot override or influence the decision. Direct the customer to Truemed support. |
| "Can I use my HSA/FSA card directly?" | Truemed handles HSA/FSA payment processing at checkout. Customers select "Pay with Truemed" rather than entering their HSA/FSA card number directly. |
| "Which of your products are eligible?" | Direct them to your HSA/FSA product collection or landing page. Do not make eligibility claims about specific products beyond what Truemed has approved. |
