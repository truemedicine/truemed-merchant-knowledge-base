## What Is Truemed?

Truemed enables qualified customers to use their HSA (Health Savings Account) and FSA (Flexible Spending Account) funds on eligible health and wellness products. We connect customers with licensed healthcare providers who evaluate eligibility and issue Letters of Medical Necessity (LMNs) to qualifying individuals.

Because HSA/FSA accounts use pre-tax dollars, your customers can save an average of 30% on eligible purchases. That means more buying power, less price sensitivity, and stronger retention.

> *HSA/FSA tax savings vary. Learn more at [truemed.com/disclosures](https://www.truemed.com/disclosures).*

## How Truemed Works

At a high level, the Truemed experience follows this flow for your customers:

1. **Discovery:** The customer sees HSA/FSA eligibility messaging on your site (product pages, banners, landing pages).
2. **Checkout or post-purchase:** Depending on your integration, the customer either pays with HSA/FSA at checkout or completes a qualification form after purchase.
3. **Health intake:** The customer completes a brief clinical intake form (~2 minutes).
4. **Provider review:** A licensed provider reviews the intake and, if appropriate, issues a Letter of Medical Necessity (LMN) within 24-48 hours.
5. **Payment or reimbursement:** The customer either pays directly with their HSA/FSA card or submits their LMN and receipt to their administrator for reimbursement.

Each LMN covers the customer's eligible purchases for a **full year**, so they only need to qualify once annually.

## Integration Types

Truemed offers two integration paths. Your Truemed contact will recommend the right one based on your storefront and business model.

### Payment Integration

For merchants on **Shopify** or **WooCommerce**, Truemed integrates directly into your checkout as a payment method. Customers select "Pay with Truemed" at checkout and can use their HSA/FSA card to pay.

Best for: Merchants with a Shopify or WooCommerce storefront who want a seamless checkout experience.

### Reimbursement Integration

For merchants on **any platform** (including Shopify), Truemed provides a post-purchase qualification link. Customers complete a health intake form after checkout, and if eligible, receive an LMN they can use to submit for HSA/FSA reimbursement.

Best for: Subscription-based businesses, merchants on platforms other than Shopify/WooCommerce, or merchants who want to offer reimbursement alongside direct payment.

<Tip>
Many Shopify merchants use **both** integrations: the payment app for one-time purchases and the reimbursement flow for subscription orders.
</Tip>

## Frequently Asked Questions

**What is an LMN?**
A Letter of Medical Necessity is a document from a licensed provider explaining why a product is medically necessary for a specific individual. It's required to use HSA/FSA funds on products that aren't automatically IRS-eligible.

**Do customers need a doctor's prescription?**
No. Truemed's network of licensed providers handles the evaluation and LMN issuance through a brief online health intake form.

**Which products are eligible?**
Eligibility is determined by Truemed's compliance team based on IRS guidelines. During onboarding, we review your full product catalog and categorize each item. Your CSM will share the approved list with you.

**Is Truemed available outside the US?**
Truemed is currently available for US-based customers with HSA or FSA accounts. Your business does not need to be US-based, but customers must have a US HSA/FSA to use Truemed.

**How does Truemed make money?**
Truemed's fee structure depends on your integration type. Your CSM will walk you through pricing during your kickoff call.
