## What Is Truemed?

Truemed partners with merchants to enable qualified customers to use their HSA/FSA funds on eligible health and wellness products. We connect customers with licensed healthcare providers who evaluate eligibility and issue Letters of Medical Necessity (LMNs) to qualifying individuals.

Because HSA/FSA accounts use pre-tax dollars, your customers can save an average of 30% on eligible purchases. That means more buying power, less price sensitivity, and stronger retention.

> HSA/FSA tax savings vary. Learn more at [truemed.com/disclosures](https://www.truemed.com/disclosures).

## How Truemed Works on WooCommerce

Truemed integrates with WooCommerce through a dedicated plugin. Here's the customer experience at a high level:

1. **Product page:** Customers see an HSA/FSA eligibility widget on your product pages, letting them know they may qualify.
2. **Checkout:** The customer selects "Truemed" as their payment method.
3. **Health intake:** After placing the order, the customer completes a health survey (~2 minutes).
4. **Provider review:** A licensed provider reviews the intake. If eligible, an LMN is issued within 24-48 hours.
5. **Payment processed:** If the customer paid with their HSA/FSA card, they're all set. If they used a regular card, they receive reimbursement instructions along with their LMN.

## Before You Get Started

Complete these steps ahead of time to keep things on track:

### 1. Upload Your Product Catalog

Provide your full product list as a CSV with the following columns: **Name**, **SKU**, and **Description**. Our team needs the complete catalog, including all product variants, to accurately categorize eligibility.

### 2. Connect Your Bank Account

Complete the Stripe Express onboarding using the link provided by your Truemed contact. See the [Stripe Onboarding & Connection](/getting-started/stripe-setup/stripe-onboarding-and-connection) article for details.

### 3. Ensure Admin Access

You'll need WordPress administrator access to install and configure the Truemed plugin on your WooCommerce store.

## Frequently Asked Questions

**What is an LMN?**
A Letter of Medical Necessity is a document from a licensed provider explaining why a product is medically necessary. It's required to use HSA/FSA funds on products that aren't automatically IRS-eligible.

**Do customers need a doctor's prescription?**
No. Truemed's network of licensed providers handles the evaluation and LMN issuance.

**Does the customer's checkout email need to match their HSA/FSA account?**
No, but the customer's name should match across the order, their Truemed health survey, and their HSA/FSA account.

**Can customers use a regular credit card and get reimbursed later?**
Yes. Customers can select the Truemed payment option and enter a non-HSA/FSA card. They'll receive reimbursement instructions along with their LMN.

**How does Truemed handle sales tax?**
Sales tax is still charged and is eligible to be paid by HSA/FSA funds.
