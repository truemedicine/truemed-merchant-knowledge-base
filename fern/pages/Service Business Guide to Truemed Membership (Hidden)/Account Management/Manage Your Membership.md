### Subscription Details

- **Annual Fee**: $500
- **Billing Cycle**: Annually, on the same day of each year as your initial sign-up.

### Cancellation Policy

We understand that business needs can change. Truemed merchants can cancel their subscriptions at any time. To ensure a smooth process, please note the following:

- **Notice Period**: Provide at least two weeks’ notice prior to your next billing cycle.
- **How to Cancel**: Send an email to **merchants@truemed.com** with your request to cancel. Be sure to include your business name, email, and any relevant account details to help us process your request quickly.

### Need Assistance?

If you have any questions or need support with your subscription, feel free to reach out to our team. You can email us at **merchants@truemed.com**, and we’ll be happy to assist.
