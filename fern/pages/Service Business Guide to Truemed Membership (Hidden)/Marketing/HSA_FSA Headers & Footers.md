## **Where to Add Messaging**

We recommend adding HSA/FSA messaging to the **top or bottom of every email template**, including:

- **Welcome emails**
- **Abandoned cart emails**
- **Browse abandonment**
- **Post-purchase emails**
- **Promotions and newsletters**
- **Winback and re-engagement campaigns**
- **Subscription renewal/reminders**

> **Tip:** Add this to your email template footer or create a reusable content block for easy use across campaigns.
