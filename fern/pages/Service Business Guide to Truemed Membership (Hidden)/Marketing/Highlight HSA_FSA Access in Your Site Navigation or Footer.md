![](https://cdn.applied.guide/media/886214b8-01bd-4c9d-8591-1d6ccbcc8c0e/5472def7-df63-4f6a-9f33-08c99d1d3e8f.png)## **The Goal**

Ensure customers can always find your Truemed **qualification survey** or [**landing page**](https://support.truemed.com/service-business/hsa-fsa-promotion-seasonal-planning) by making HSA/FSA access a permanent part of your site’s navigation or footer.

A simple, visible placement can be one of the highest-impact changes you make. Gyms and studios have doubled their Truemed conversions within a month after adding this update. Placing HSA/FSA alongside “Resources,” “Membership,” or “Pricing” ensures every visitor knows they can pay with pre-tax dollars.

### **Why It Works:**

- **Always visible:** Customers can discover eligibility at any point in their journey

- **Low lift:** Quick update in your CMS or website builder (\~10 minutes)

- **Proven impact:** Captures customers who might otherwise miss HSA/FSA messaging

## **How to Add HSA/FSA to Your Homepage Nav:**

### **1. Choose a placement:**

- **Footer:** under “Resources” or “Help”

- **Navigation menu:** under “Membership,” “Pricing,” or “Plans”


- **Destination:** Goes directly to your Truemed **landing page** or **qualification survey**

### **2. Pick A Label**

Use the option that best fits your brand voice:

- HSA/FSA Eligibility

- Qualify to Use HSA/FSA

- HSA/FSA Savings

### **3. Set The Destination**

Point the placement directly to your Truemed [**Truemed landing page**](https://support.truemed.com/service-business/hsa-fsa-promotion-seasonal-planning) or **qualification survey** so customers reach the right page every time.
